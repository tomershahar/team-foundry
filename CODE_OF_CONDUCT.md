# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be direct, honest, and kind. Treat everyone with respect. Disagree with ideas, not people.

## What this means in practice

- Give feedback on the work, not the person
- Say what you mean; ask what you don't understand
- Assume good intent until there's evidence otherwise
- Make space for people who are newer to this than you

## What we won't tolerate

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Reporting

If something goes wrong, email the maintainer directly. Reports are confidential.

## Enforcement

Violations result in a warning, then removal from the project. Serious violations skip the warning.

---

This code of conduct is short on purpose. The standard is respect. Use your judgment.
