# ADR-004 — Staging VPC shares prod VPC (accepted risk)

**Date:** 2024-11-14 | **Decided by:** Marcus | **Status:** Active — under review

## Context

We evaluated full VPC separation for staging vs. prod. The cost and complexity were significant for our team size.

## Decision

Staging uses the same VPC as prod, with separate security groups. This is a known risk.

## Rationale

At our current scale, the probability of a staging incident affecting prod is low and the cost is acceptable. This decision will be revisited at 250 customers.

## Status note

Under review — Priya has flagged that as we approach 200 customers, this risk is rising. Scheduled for architectural review in Q3 2026.
